Hinduism

Methods To Ward Off

Hinduism has this calling for supernatural creature as "Bhoot". They believe that bhoots are supposed to fear water and steel or iron objects. The scent of burnt turmeric and invoking the name of holy figures and deities is also said to repel bhoots. In some regions, sprinkling earth on oneself is said to shield against bhoots. According to Hindu mythology, the bhoot is just a deceased, lost or angry soul that cannot be destroyed by any means. Rituals are performed by those haunted by the bhoot to promise it that everything in the power of them would be done to either assure the rebirth of the bhoot or finish the works left incomplete by the bhoot. With that, the bhoot gets what it wants and would stop troubling those haunted by it forever.
